FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about registration, voting, security and results in this online voting system.

General

Who is eligible to vote?
All students listed on the official voter roll for this election (provided by your institution) are eligible to vote once the voting window opens.
Can I change my vote after submitting?
In most elections, you cannot change your vote once the ballot is submitted. The rules for your specific election are defined by your institution and will be clearly communicated before you vote.
Is this website connected to a real database?
This project is a front‑end demo only. It showcases the user interface for an online voting system using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery without persisting real data.

Security & privacy

How is my vote kept anonymous?
In a real deployment, your identity is verified before you vote, then separated from your encrypted ballot. The system stores your vote and your identity in different locations and never links them together.
Is my connection secure?
Elections should always run over HTTPS with strong encryption, HSTS enabled and modern security headers to protect your data in transit.
Who can view the results?
Aggregated election results are typically public, but access to detailed breakdowns and audit logs is limited to authorized officials and external auditors where applicable.
