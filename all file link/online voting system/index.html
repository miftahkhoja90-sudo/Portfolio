Process

How secure online voting works

A simple four‑step flow that keeps ballots private while ensuring the integrity of every vote.

01

Register & verify identity

Create your voter account, verify your email, and complete identity checks configured by your institution.

02

Review candidates

Explore candidate manifestos, party affiliations, and experience before casting your ballot.

03

Cast your encrypted ballot

Submit your vote from any device. Ballots are encrypted client‑side and stored anonymously.

04

Instant verifiable results

Once polls close, results are tallied and published instantly with an auditable trail.
