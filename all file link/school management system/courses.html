Courses & Class Structure

From foundational skills to advanced streams, our curriculum is designed to support every learner.

School sections

Each section is carefully structured to meet the developmental needs of its learners.

Pre-primary & Primary (KG — Grade 5)

Focus on foundational literacy, numeracy, motor skills, and socio-emotional learning through play-based and activity-based methods.

Middle School (Grades 6 — 8)

Strong conceptual understanding across Science, Mathematics, Social Sciences, and languages with exposure to coding and robotics.

Senior School (Grades 9 — 12)

Stream-based learning (Science, Commerce, Humanities) with career counseling, board exam preparation, and competitive exam support.
